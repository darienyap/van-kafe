Our Story

A little corner that
feels like home

Vän Kafé was born from more than just a love for good coffee and food — it was built on the foundation of friendship. The name "Vän," meaning "friend" in Swedish, reflects the heart of who we are: a space created to bring people together.

Founded by Vän Hus Interior, Vän Kafé began as a dream shared among friends — friends who believed that design, comfort, and community could live in the same space. With a passion for aesthetics and a deep appreciation for meaningful connection, we set out to create a café that felt like a second home.

Our menu is crafted with the same care — celebrating quality ingredients, bold flavors, and the joy of sharing a meal or a cup of coffee with someone special. We're more than just a café. We're a community. A space where friendships begin, stories are shared, and time slows down just enough for you to savor what really matters.
